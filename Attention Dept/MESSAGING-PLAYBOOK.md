# Attention Dept: Messaging Playbook

Use this guide to maintain consistency when updating copy, adding case studies, or creating marketing materials.

---

## Core Positioning

**What we are:** Growth systems agency (not creative agency, not consulting firm, not tech company)

**What we do:** Build content distribution + conversion systems that turn organic attention into paying customers

**What makes us different:** We focus on the full loop (attention → conversion → scale), not just vanity metrics

---

## The 3-Word Test

Every piece of copy must be explainable in 3 words. Examples:

✅ "Viral content distribution" (specific, clear)  
✅ "Conversion funnels" (everyone understands)  
✅ "Weekly optimization" (actionable)  

❌ "Attention infrastructure" (vague, jargon)  
❌ "Engineered systems" (unclear implementation)  
❌ "Algorithmic scaling" (meaningless to business owners)  

---

## The Headline Formula

**Problem + Solution + Outcome**

Example: "Viral attention that converts to customers."
- Problem: You have no reach
- Solution: We create viral content
- Outcome: You get customers

---

## Forbidden Words (Use Alternatives Instead)

| **Forbidden** | **Use Instead** | **Why** |
|---|---|---|
| infrastructure | system, setup, process | "Infrastructure" is too abstract |
| engineered | built, designed, created | "Engineered" sounds like tech jargon |
| algorithmic scaling | organic reach, distributed content | "Algorithmic" is imprecise and sounds like BS |
| momentum | growth, traction, results | "Momentum" is unprovable |
| signal | reach, followers, engagement | "Signal" is abstract |
| leverage | tool, system, capability | "Leverage" is overused corporate speak |
| architecture | structure, plan, strategy | "Architecture" is vague |

## What NOT to Claim

- "Guaranteed virality" (no one can guarantee this)
- "Overnight growth" (undermines credibility)
- "Works for everyone" (it doesn't—we have ICPs)
- "No work required" (clients need to invest 3–6 months)
- "Unique algorithm hack" (we don't have secret sauce)

---

## What TO Claim

- "50+ brands scaled" (proven track record)
- "200M+ organic views" (real result)
- "10→200+ sales" (specific outcome)
- "3–6 month deployments" (transparent timeline)
- "Done-with-you systems" (clear partnership model)

## Tone Guidelines

**We are:**
- Direct (no flowery language)
- Confident (we know what works)
- Transparent (we show our work)
- Action-oriented (we move fast)

**We are NOT:**
- Salesy (no hype)
- Mysterious (no vague promises)
- Corporate (no jargon)
- Arrogant (we're humble about what works)

---

## CTA Language

**Primary:** "Apply for a Growth System"  
(Why: Clear action, specific outcome)

**Secondary:** "View Case Studies" / "See Case Studies"  
(Why: Social proof, not passive "explore")

**Tertiary:** "Let's grow together" (footer emphasis)  
(Why: Reinforces partnership)

**Avoid:** "Request a consultation" (too generic), "Book a call" (no commitment), "Learn more" (passive)

---

## Metric Framing

**Old way:** "300+ systems built" (activity metric)  
**New way:** "50+ brands scaled" (outcome metric, client-focused)

**Old way:** "200M+ organic impressions"  
**New way:** "200M+ organic views" (simpler, same metric)

**Old way:** "Sales growth 10x"  
**New way:** "10→200+ sales per client" (specific, credible)

## Forbidden Phrases (Don't Use These)

- "growth hacking" (meaningless)
- "ninja/guru/expert" (cringe)
- "best practices" (vague)
- "cutting-edge technology" (we don't build tech)
- "transformative" (overused)
- "paradigm shift" (corporate BS)
- "in this day and age" (filler)
- "let's synergize" (parody territory)
- "move the needle" (cliché)

---

## Quality Checklist for Any Copy

Before publishing, ask:

- [ ] Can a business owner understand this in 3–5 seconds?
- [ ] Does it explain WHAT we do (not just WHY)?
- [ ] Are there any vague words like "infrastructure," "engineered," "algorithmic"?
- [ ] Is there a specific next step (not "learn more")?
- [ ] Does it feel honest (not overpromising)?
- [ ] Is it action-focused (verbs, not adjectives)?
- [ ] Does it mention a specific platform (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) or timeframe?
